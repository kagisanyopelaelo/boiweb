Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?
Yes, we recommend scheduling one online or by phone.

Do you accept walk-ins?
Limited walk-ins are accepted based on availability.

How often should I get a check-up?
Every 6 months, or as recommended by your dentist.

Insurance & Claims

We accept the following medical aid schemes:

  • B'pomas
  • Bomaid
  • Pula medical aid
  • Botsogo health plan

For claims, please ensure you bring a valid membership card and ID. We will assist with direct claims where possible. Co-payments may apply for some treatments.
