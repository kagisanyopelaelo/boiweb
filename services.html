General Dentistry

At Boipelo Dental Clinic, our general dentistry services form the foundation of your long-term oral health. We offer comprehensive preventive and diagnostic care tailored to patients of all ages. Whether you're coming in for a routine check-up or you're experiencing discomfort, our team is here to ensure your smile stays healthy.

Our General Services Include:

  • Oral Examinations: Complete assessment of teeth, gums, and mouth to detect any early signs of issues.
  • Dental Cleanings: Removal of plaque and tartar to prevent gum disease and cavities.
  • Tooth Fillings: Durable, natural-looking fillings to treat and seal cavities.
  • X-rays & Diagnostics: High-quality imaging for accurate detection of hidden problems.
  • Fluoride Treatments: Strengthens tooth enamel and protects against decay.
  • Patient Education: Tips and guidance on brushing, flossing, and diet for long-term health.

Our friendly team uses the latest technology and gentle techniques to make sure every visit is as comfortable as possible. We believe in building lasting relationships with our patients and working together to achieve optimal dental health.

Your journey to a brighter, healthier smile starts here!

Pediatric Dentistry

Gentle and fun care for kids, from baby teeth to teens. We make every visit stress-free.

Our pediatric dental services are designed to make every child feel safe, comfortable, and even excited about visiting the dentist! At Boipelo Dental Clinic, we specialize in providing gentle and friendly care tailored to the unique needs of infants, children, and teenagers.

We Offer:

  • First Dental Visits: Gentle exams for babies and toddlers to introduce them to the dental chair.
  • Preventive Cleanings & Fluoride: Keep little teeth strong and cavity-free.
  • Sealants: Protective coatings to shield molars from decay.
  • Tooth-Colored Fillings: Kid-friendly, natural-looking fillings for small cavities.
  • Habit Counseling: Guidance for thumb-sucking, pacifier use, and teething care.
  • Emergency Care: Prompt attention to dental injuries or sudden pain.

We understand that a positive early dental experience can shape a lifetime of healthy habits. That’s why we focus on education, patience, and creating a fun environment — complete with friendly staff and a calm setting.

Your child’s smile deserves a bright, healthy start — and we’re here to make it happen!

Restorative Treatments

From fillings to crowns and bridges, we repair and restore damaged or missing teeth.

At Boipelo Dental Clinic, we offer restorative dentistry solutions to repair damaged, decayed, or missing teeth. Our goal is to restore both the function and beauty of your smile, using techniques that are durable, safe, and aesthetically pleasing.

Our Restorative Services Include:

  • Dental Fillings: Treat cavities with tooth-colored materials that blend in naturally.
  • Dental Crowns: Strengthen and protect weakened or broken teeth with custom-made caps.
  • Bridges: Replace one or more missing teeth using secure, fixed dental bridges.
  • Dentures: Full and partial removable dentures for patients with multiple missing teeth.
  • Root Canal Therapy: Save infected teeth by removing the pulp and sealing the root.
  • Tooth Extraction: Safe removal of damaged or impacted teeth when necessary.

Every treatment is carefully planned with your comfort and oral health in mind. We use modern materials and technology to ensure long-lasting results that feel as good as they look.

Let us help you regain confidence in your smile — one tooth at a time!

Orthodontic Services

At Boipelo Dental Clinic, our orthodontic services are tailored to align your teeth and jaw for a healthier, more confident smile. Whether you’re a teen or an adult, we offer personalized solutions to suit your lifestyle and dental goals.

Our Orthodontic Services Include:

  • Braces: Traditional metal braces for reliable, time-tested teeth alignment.
  • Ceramic Braces: Tooth-colored brackets for a more subtle look during treatment.
  • Clear Aligners: Removable, nearly invisible trays for comfortable, discreet straightening.
  • Retainers: Custom-fitted devices to maintain your new smile after treatment.
  • Jaw Alignment Therapy: Correct bite problems (malocclusion) for better function and appearance.

We work closely with our patients throughout the entire orthodontic journey — from consultation to aftercare — ensuring you're informed and comfortable every step of the way.

Straighter teeth aren’t just beautiful — they’re healthier, too!
